About Me

My name is Karol, and I currently work as an assistant to the quality manager in a manufacturing plant.

I am a licensed Chemical Engineer and Chemical Technician, having earned my Bachelor's degree in Chemical Engineering from UP Diliman.

I am pursuing this Diploma Course in Computer Science at UPOU to build a strong foundation in computer science and artificial intelligence as these are rapidly evolving and impactful fields.

In the future, I plan to further my knowledge by pursuing a Master's degree in either Computer Science or Data Science when the opportunity arises.
